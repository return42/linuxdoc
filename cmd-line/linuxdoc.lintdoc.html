linuxdoc.lintdoc¶

Lint the kernel-doc comments in the source code. E.g. to lint the kernel-doc comments of a source-file.:

$ linuxdoc.lintdoc /share/linux/include/media/lirc_dev.h

Alternative you could also lint whole parts of the source tree, e.g. the include folder:

$ linuxdoc.lintdoc /share/linux/include/
